My Preferred Tea of Choice

My preferred tea of choice would have to be ginger tea. Ginger tea is not only soothing
to drink, but also offers many benefits. One of it's many benefits includes digestive aid,
which means it can help with the digestion of food and the absorbtion and processing of
micronutrients to the body. For this reason ginger tea is my preferred choice of drink,
especially after a meal!
